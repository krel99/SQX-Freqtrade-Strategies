Strategy: VolSqueezeBreakout_MultiFrame
Timeframes: 5m (base), 1h (secondary)

Objective: Identify a volatility squeeze on a higher timeframe (1h) and enter a breakout trade on a lower timeframe (5m) when momentum indicators align.

Indicators (1h):
- Bollinger Bands (period_bb_1h, stddev_bb_1h)
- Keltner Channels (ema_period_kc_1h, atr_period_kc_1h, mult_kc_1h)
- Ichimoku Cloud (senkou_span_b_period_1h, tenkan_sen_period_1h, kijun_sen_period_1h)

Indicators (5m):
- Bollinger Bands (period_bb_5m, stddev_bb_5m)
- RSI (period_rsi_5m)
- MACD (fast_macd_5m, slow_macd_5m, signal_macd_5m)
- ADX (period_adx_5m)
- Volume MA (period_volume_ma_5m)

Entry Conditions (Long):
1.  **1h Volatility Squeeze**:
    - The 1h Bollinger Bands are inside the 1h Keltner Channels.
    - Price is above the 1h Ichimoku Cloud (Senkou Span A and B).
2.  **5m Breakout**:
    - The volatility squeeze on the 1h timeframe has recently ended (e.g., within the last 3-5 candles).
    - Price on the 5m timeframe breaks above the upper Bollinger Band.
3.  **5m Momentum Confirmation**:
    - RSI_5m > rsi_threshold_5m
    - MACD_5m > MACD_signal_5m
    - ADX_5m > adx_threshold_5m
    - Volume_5m > Volume_MA_5m * volume_spike_factor_5m

Exit Conditions (Long):
1.  **Overbought Signal**:
    - RSI_5m > rsi_exit_threshold_5m
2.  **Price falls below 5m Bollinger Band middle line**:
    - `close` < `bb_middleband_5m`
3.  **Trailing Stop Loss**:
    - A trailing stop loss is triggered if the price moves against the trade.
