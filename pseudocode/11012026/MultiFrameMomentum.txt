Strategy: MultiFrameMomentum
Timeframes: 15m (base), 1h (secondary)

Objective: Enter trades when momentum indicators on both timeframes align, and exit on overbought signals or a trailing stop loss.

Indicators (15m):
- RSI (period_15m)
- MACD (fast_15m, slow_15m, signal_15m)
- Bollinger Bands (period_bb_15m, stddev_bb_15m)
- Awesome Oscillator (ao_15m)

Indicators (1h):
- RSI (period_1h)
- MACD (fast_1h, slow_1h, signal_1h)
- Bollinger Bands (period_bb_1h, stddev_bb_1h)

Entry Conditions (Long):
1.  **1h Momentum**:
    - RSI_1h > rsi_threshold_1h
    - MACD_1h > MACD_signal_1h
    - Price is above the middle Bollinger Band on the 1h timeframe.
2.  **15m Momentum**:
    - RSI_15m > rsi_threshold_15m
    - MACD_15m > MACD_signal_15m
    - Awesome Oscillator > 0
3.  **15m Entry Trigger**:
    - Price crosses above the upper Bollinger Band on the 15m timeframe.

Exit Conditions (Long):
1.  **Overbought Signal**:
    - RSI_15m > rsi_exit_threshold_15m
2.  **Trailing Stop Loss**:
    - A trailing stop loss is triggered if the price moves against the trade.
